TRUSTist REVIEWer plugin adds a shortcode and widgets on your site site showing your customer reviews and star rating.

== Description ==

TRUSTist REVIEWer plugin adds a widget to your website.

* Get more clicks and make more sales by getting stars in your natural search results in Google.
* Aggregate your reviews from all review sources such as Google, Facebook and anywhere else you have them.
* Display your reviews from all around the web and your star rating widget on your website.
* Users can leave a review directly from your website or using a unique TRUSTist link if you would prefer to collect reviews by invitation only.
* Manually or automatically create posts onto your social media platforms using your 4 and 5 star reviews.

TRUSTist will provide the relevant codes to be used with the plugin via email. You must have a TRUSTist account in order to use this plugin. Please call +44 (0) 1904 217 110 to set one up.


== Installation ==

Upload the TRUSTist REVIEWer plugin to your blog, Activate it, then enter your TRUSTist REVIEWer Brand Code and Location Code.  Please call +44 (0) 1904 217 110 to set up your account.

1, 2, 3: You're done!
